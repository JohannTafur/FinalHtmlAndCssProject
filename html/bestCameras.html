Best Camaras

  1. CANON EOS 850D

    • Camera with manual controls

    • interchangeable objectives

    • Good autofocus system (Dual Pixel CMOS)

    • Flip-up touch screen, ideal for Vlogging

    • Video recording in 4K (25fps)

    • External microphone input

    • No in-body stabilizer (integrates into lenses)

    • 30 minute recording limit per clip

    • Weight: 515g. approx.

    • WIFI

  2. NIKON D7500

    • Camera with manual controls
    • interchangeable objectives
    • Record in 4k at 60p
    • audio input
    • headphone output
    • Salida HDMI
    • WIFI
    • Weight 720g.

  3. OLYMPUS OM-D E-M10 MARK IV

    • manual controls
    • interchangeable objectives
    • Sensor micro 4/3
    • Tactile and tiltable LCD display
    • Video in 4K at 30 fps and fullHD (1080p) at 60 fps
    • 5-axis stabilizer on the body
    • Maximum recording per clip of 29 minutes
    • WIFI
    • Approximate weight 383gr (not counting the lens).

  4. SONY A6600

    • Integrated 5-axis stabilizer
    • 4K recording at 30 fps
    • At 1080p you can record up to 120 fps
    • folding screen
    • Fast, reliable autofocus with face detection
    • Good response to high ISOs
    • audio input
    • headphone output
    • Full HD slow motion (up to 120fps)

  5. SONY A7 III

    • manual controls
    • interchangeable objectives
    • Full Frame
    • High precision autofocus
    • Record in 4K at 60p
    • double groove
    • 5-axis stabilizer on the body
    • Touch screen (adjustable only upwards to approx. 107º, downwards to approx. 41º)
    • Sealed against dust and humidity.
    • Approximate weight 650gr.

  6. NIKON Z6

    • Camera with manual controls
    • interchangeable objectives
    • Precise autofocus with eye detection
    • 5-axis in-body stabilization
    • Record in 4k at 30p
    • Very good behavior in low light environments
    • Flip-up LCD touch screen
    • sealed body
    • 1 card slot
    • audio input
    • headphone output
    • WIFI

  7. FUJIFILM X-T4

    • Double card slot
    • In-body stabilizer
    • folding screen
    • Good autofocus performance even in low light situations
    • Wi-Fi connectivity
    • Light and compact with only 607gr. of weight

  8. PANASONIC LUMIX GH5S

    • Sealed against dust and splashes
    • Good response to high ISOs
    • integrated stabilizer
    • folding screen
    • 4K a 60fps
    • unlimited recording
    • audio input
    • headphone output
    • WIFI
    • Weight 660g. with battery and card included.

  9. NIKON Z50

    • Flip and touch screen

    • audio input
    • WIFI
    • Peso 450gr. aprox.
